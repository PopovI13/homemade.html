🛰️ Galactic Survival Guide

Welcome, traveler. Whether you’re a smuggler dodging bounty hunters or a moisture farmer avoiding sand people, this guide will help you survive the Star Wars galaxy (or at least die with style).

🌍 Essential Tips

  • Never tell anyone you have a “bad feeling about this.” It’s basically a death sentence.
  • If a lightsaber turns red — run. Don’t ask questions. Just run.
  • Don’t trust droids who “just want to help.” Especially protocol droids. They talk too much.
  • Jedi robes don’t protect you from blasters. Or lava. Don’t try it.
Survival Tip: If someone offers you blue milk, accept it. If it's green... maybe ask what planet you're on first.

💥 Places to Avoid (Unless You're Into That)

  • 🌋 Mustafar – Volcanoes, lava rivers, and angry Sith. 0/10 vacation spot.
  • ❄️ Hoth – Cold enough to freeze your tauntaun before you even unpack.
  • 🌪️ Kessel – You might make the run in under 12 parsecs, but your social life won’t.
  • 🎰 Canto Bight – Rich, fancy, boring. Unless you like space casinos and getting arrested.
Pro Tip: Tattooine may look cheap, but between the twin suns and the crime rate, it’s basically space Florida.

🧑‍🚀 Who NOT to Sit Next To on a Starship

  • Chewbacca – Great guy. Bad at armrests. Worse if you beat him at holochess.
  • Any Sith Lord – You’ll never finish your drink. Or your sentence.
  • A Clone Trooper who says “I feel weird lately.” Just… no.
  • Yoda – Will judge your snack choices. Backwards, he'll talk. Annoying, it becomes.

🎒 Packing List

  • Blaster (set to stun — unless you're feeling spicy)
  • One clean robe (because Jedi always travel light)
  • Snack bars (you never know when you'll be stuck in hyperspace traffic)
  • Translator droid — unless you speak 6 million forms of communication
